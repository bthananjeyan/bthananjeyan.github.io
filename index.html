Brijen Thananjeyan

I am a Senior Research Scientist at Ambi Robotics.

Previously, I was a PhD Student at UC Berkeley in the UC Berkeley AUTOLAB, a part of the Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research (BAIR) Lab, and the Real-Time Intelligent Secure Explainable Systems (RISE) Lab, advised by Ken Goldberg and Joseph Gonzalez. I am interested in safe and efficient learning algorithms for robots.
